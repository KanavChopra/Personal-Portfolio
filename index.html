Hi, I'm Kanav Chopra

I am a passionate C++ coder, Front-End developer and Tech-Enthusiast

Hi, my name is Kanav Chopra. I am a 3rd year student of Computer Science and Engineering at Chitkara University Institute of Information and Technology, Rajpura, Punjab. I am interested in Web Development mainly ReactJS. Pursuing CSE hase repeatedly exposed me to a new culture and thorugh this I am fortunate enough to meet diverse group of people. This has made me able to quickly adapt to changes and gain a broader perspective. I am always curious about things and enjoy learning. I like to solve problems and fix errors. I am a detail oriented person and keep my goals and tasks organised to maintain productivity.

I am hardworking and dedicated team player passionate about implementing and launching new projects. Ever since I wrote my first program in Python, I have been obsessed with the idea of using software to solve practical problems. Software Engineering is a never-ending puzzle that I am passionately engaged in solving. I believe in the power of programming to transform and improve the lives of people around the world. I believe that the skills that I have attained from the dynamic environment and competitive university life define me and I am looking forward to implemementing and polishing them in any opportunity that I receive.
